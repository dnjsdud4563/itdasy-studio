# Privacy Policy

Effective: April 16, 2026.

## 1. Data We Collect
- **Required**: Email, OAuth provider ID (Apple/Google/Kakao), nickname
- **Optional**: Real name, phone number (encrypted with **AES-256 GCM** before storage)
- **Automatic**: Hashed IP address, User-Agent, access path/time
- **Instagram** (if connected): Username, profile picture, media feed (accessed via Meta Graph API)

## 2. How We Use Your Data
- Account identification, service delivery, payment/subscription management
- AI image generation (prompts sent to image generation service; no PII transmitted)
- Instagram feed display within the app (when user explicitly connects their account)
- Fraud prevention and legal compliance

## 3. Data Retention & Deletion
- **Account data**: Deleted 30 days after account deletion request
- **Access logs**: Retained for 90 days per Korean Telecommunications Act, then automatically purged
- **Payment records**: Retained for 5 years per Korean E-Commerce Act
- **Instagram data**: Deleted immediately upon disconnection or via Meta Data Deletion Callback

## 4. Third-Party Processors
- **Supabase** (Authentication, Database) — Singapore/US
- **Meta Platforms** (Instagram Graph API) — US
- **Pollinations/Ideogram** (AI Image Generation) — prompts only, no PII
- **RevenueCat / PortOne** (Payment Processing)

## 5. Your Rights
- Right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data
- In-app "Export My Data" and "Delete Account" features
- Instagram disconnection available at any time

## 6. California Residents (CCPA)
- You have the right to know what personal information we collect and request its deletion
- We do not sell personal information
- Contact: privacy@beauty-platform.example.com

## 7. Artificial Intelligence Transparency (Korean AI Basic Act / EU AI Act)
- This service uses **generative AI** to create and process images.
- Models used: Pollinations AI (image generation), RMBG-2.0 (background removal)
- All AI-generated content is labeled with an **"AI Generated" badge** and metadata tag.
- Users are notified **before** using AI features that the function is powered by artificial intelligence.
- AI results are **not medical diagnoses** and do not replace professional consultation.
- You are interacting with an **AI system**, not a human expert, when using analysis features.
